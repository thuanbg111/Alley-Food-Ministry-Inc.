ALLEY FOOD MINISTRY, INC.

We are a California 501(c)(3) nonprofit public benefit corporation. Our specific purpose is to provide charitable food relief to the poor and distressed or to the underprivileged, serving families with dignity and compassion.

Learn more See programs

What We Do

Practical food assistance for neighbors in need, delivered with care.

  • Weekly pantry distribution & grocery boxes
  • Mobile outreach to reach the unhoused and homebound
  • Partnerships with local organizations & donors

Nutrition & Education

Guides and workshops on healthy, budget‑friendly meals.

Emergency Food

Rapid response support for families in crisis situations.

Community Partners

We collaborate with food banks, markets, and faith groups.
